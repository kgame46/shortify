Frequently asked questions

How long can my original video be?

Our AI is optimised for videos up to 2 hours long. For extremely long files, we recommend splitting them before uploading.

Will my short have a watermark?

Free users will see a small watermark. All paid plans export clips without any branding.

Can I edit the clips myself?

Absolutely! Download the generated short and further refine it in your favourite editing tool or upload it back to Shortify for a new variation.
